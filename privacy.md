
# Privacy Policy

**Vellore — Watch Collection Manager**
Last updated: March 5, 2026

## Overview

Vellore is designed with privacy in mind. Your data stays on your device and in your personal iCloud account. We do not collect, track, or share any personal information.

## Data Storage

All data you enter into Vellore — including your watch collection, wear logs, service records, accuracy readings, photos, wishlist items, and app settings — is stored locally on your device and synced to your personal iCloud account via Apple's CloudKit service.

We do not operate any servers. We have no access to your data.

## iCloud Sync

If you have iCloud enabled on your device, Vellore uses Apple's CloudKit to sync your data across your devices. This data is stored in your personal iCloud account and is governed by Apple's privacy policy. You can disable iCloud sync for Vellore in your device's iCloud settings at any time.

## Data Collection

Vellore does not collect:
- Personal information (name, email, phone number)
- Location data
- Usage analytics or behavioral data
- Device identifiers for tracking purposes
- Any data from other apps on your device

## Third-Party Services

Vellore does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services. The only external service used is Apple's StoreKit for processing in-app purchases, which is governed by Apple's privacy policy.

## In-App Purchases

Vellore offers a one-time lifetime Pro upgrade processed entirely through Apple's App Store. We do not receive or store any payment information.

## Photos

Photos you add to your watch collection are stored on your device and in your iCloud account. Vellore accesses your photo library only when you explicitly choose to add a photo. Photos are never transmitted to us or any third party.

## Children's Privacy

Vellore does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The app does not require an account and does not collect personal data from any user.

## Data Deletion

You can delete all your data at any time from within the app via Settings. Uninstalling the app removes all locally stored data. To remove iCloud data, use your device's iCloud storage settings.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
